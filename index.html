Towards the chat among distinct LLM agents
CS3966: Natural Language Processing and Large Language Model, Spring 2024
paper / slides

We investigated the collaborative potential of completely distinct LLM agents (GPT, Llama, and Wenxin Yiyan) through structured dialogues and wrote a paper about the results. Surprisingly, we discovered a new technique termed ”Virtual Conversation”, where an LLM internally simulates a multi-agent discussion.
